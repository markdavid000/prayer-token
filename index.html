ABOUT SEKIN CX

What is Sekin CX?

Sekin CX is complete fully automated and seamless data processing, data analytic and blockchain based rewarding platform, with dynamic dashboard that responds to data collected via surveys. Hence, Sekin CX is a method, process and systems that analyze the data received by survey taking by end users. It facilitates the data from survey creation, to survey taking, data collection, data processing, and data analytics to dynamic real time dashboards, and automated rewarding system by SEK ERC20 tokens based on tasks completion.

How does Sekin CX work?

Once you register and log in, you will be provided with a workstation to create a new project. You typically start with creating a new survey, and all the tools to create a new survey will be available at your finger tips. Once you complete the survey design, you will have an option to Save and Publish later, or Publish instantly. If you chose to save and Publish later, you can review your survey at a later stage and amend the survey contents at a later time. Once you chose to finalize and publish, then you can no longer be able to amend the survey content. After publishing, you will be provided with a share link, which you can copy it and paste on various other medium as you wish to campaign your survey. Hence, campaigning your survey is a key for successful inflow of data. If data doesn't flow it means that your campaigning strategy is not effective, and you will need to re-campaign until you get right flow of data.
